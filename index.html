    When Did the Resin art was discovered

    Resin art, as we know it today using synthetic resins like epoxy, emerged in the 1930s with the development of epoxy resin. While natural resins and amber have been used for various purposes for centuries, including decorative objects and jewelry, the use of synthetic epoxy resin as a primary medium in art is a more recent development. (source:Shreyashri Magazine )
